Settlement Endpoint Control Tower is a synthetic reference demo. It shows how banks could pre-validate identity-bound wallet endpoints beside BIC, LEI/vLEI-style authority, fiat SSI fallback and ISO/UETR-style visibility. It does not claim live payment execution, live payment-network integration, live legal-entity or authority verification, live wallet ownership verification or production compliance.
